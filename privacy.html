Privacy Policy

Effective immediately upon downloading Stokk. We take simulation seriously, and privacy even more seriously.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Stokk ("us", "we", or "our"), a premium simulated crypto trading terminal operating natively on Expo Go. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you practice paper-trading across the market in our application. Because our assets and bankrolls are 100% simulated, our fundamental policy is data minimalization.

2. Zero Real Financial Data

Because our application allows you to trade with fake money ("Stokk Tokens" or standard fiat simulations), we will never ask you to link a legitimate brokerage API, provide banking credentials, or expose credit card information. Your simulated portfolio balance is purely a mathematical state living in your localized app storage.

3. What We Map and Store

In order to provide competitive leaderboards and progression tiers based on your tap count, we store:

  • An anonymous installation UUID generated by Expo upon initialization.
  • Your current simulation milestones (e.g., tap count, acquired fake-crypto assets).
  • Non-identifiable crash reports to stabilize the terminal environment.

4. Third-Party Market Engines

While your money is completely simulated, to give you a hyper-authentic paper-trading environment, our application subscribes to real-time asset feeds. These feeds are strictly read-only incoming websocket packets providing global market variables. No personal data flows outward to these market engines.

5. Revisions and Contact Info

This Privacy Policy may evolve periodically as we expand simulated features. For any inquiries regarding your data mapping, contact our simulation engineers directly at pigeon.software.2012@gmail.com.
